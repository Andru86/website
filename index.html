International General Insurance (IGIC)

By John Messerli — June 8, 2025

IGIC is a global leader in insurance solutions, providing innovative products to customers worldwide. Founded over a century ago, IGIC has built a reputation for reliability and expertise.

The company offers a broad range of insurance services, including property, casualty, life, and health insurance. With a focus on customer satisfaction, IGIC continuously adapts to the changing market demands.

History

Since its establishment, IGIC has expanded its operations internationally, maintaining strong local partnerships. The company embraces digital transformation to improve service delivery.

Products & Services

IGIC provides tailored insurance products to meet individual and business needs. Their flagship products include:

  • Comprehensive property insurance
  • Customized health plans
  • Risk management consulting

With an emphasis on sustainability, IGIC invests in green insurance products that support environmental responsibility.<
